# Compound SEO Engine™

The Compound SEO Engine™ is BankDeMark’s model for building organic traffic as a long-term asset, not a one-time content campaign.

Most websites publish content as isolated pages.

That is weak SEO.

The Compound SEO Engine™ treats every article, calculator, pillar page, internal link, proof asset, and framework as part of one connected authority system.

## The Core Idea

SEO compounds when:

- one article supports another article
- cluster pages reinforce pillar pages
- informational content pushes authority toward commercial pages
- internal links create clear semantic pathways
- proof assets increase trust
- recurring frameworks create brand entities
- old content is refreshed instead of abandoned

Publishing more content is not automatically an SEO strategy.

Publishing interconnected content is.

## The Five Layers

### 1. Pillar Layer

Large authority pages that own broad topics.

Examples:
- personal finance
- investing
- business credit
- banking
- debt management
- financial freedom

### 2. Cluster Layer

Supporting articles that attack specific long-tail searches.

Examples:
- how compound interest works
- best compound interest investments
- TFSA vs RRSP
- credit card payoff strategies

### 3. Tool Layer

Calculators and interactive assets that satisfy high-intent searches.

Examples:
- compound interest calculator
- retirement calculator
- mortgage calculator
- debt payoff calculator

### 4. Proof Layer

Real screenshots, case studies, rankings, experiments, GSC data, and original observations.

This is what separates an authority publication from generic AI content.

### 5. Revenue Layer

Pages that turn attention into business value.

Examples:
- newsletter signups
- calculator usage
- partner offers
- services
- products
- brand trust

## The BankDeMark Rule

Every page must do at least one of three things:

1. capture search demand
2. support a higher-value page
3. prove real-world expertise

If a page does none of those, it does not belong in the system.
