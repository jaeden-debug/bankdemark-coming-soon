# Revenue-Weighted SEO™

Revenue-Weighted SEO™ is BankDeMark’s framework for prioritizing SEO execution based on revenue potential rather than traffic vanity metrics.

Not all traffic is valuable.

A keyword generating 500 visitors who buy is more valuable than a keyword generating 50,000 visitors who bounce.

Revenue-Weighted SEO™ prioritizes:
- commercial intent
- conversion probability
- revenue contribution
- lifetime customer value
- authority consolidation

instead of:
- vanity traffic
- broad informational reach
- low-intent keywords

---

# Core Components

## 1. Intent Weighting
Assigning higher priority to keywords with purchasing intent.

---

## 2. Revenue Mapping
Connecting articles and pages directly to monetization pathways.

---

## 3. Authority Allocation
Pushing internal authority toward revenue-driving pages.

---

## 4. Funnel Reinforcement
Using informational content to support transactional assets.

---

# Core Principle

Traffic alone is not a business model.

Revenue-generating authority is.
