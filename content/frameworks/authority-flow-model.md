# Authority Flow Model™

The Authority Flow Model™ is BankDeMark’s internal linking and semantic authority framework for distributing ranking power intentionally across a website.

Most websites leak authority.

Pages exist in isolation.
Important content gets buried.
High-performing pages fail to support commercial pages.

The Authority Flow Model™ solves this by creating directional authority movement across the site.

The model operates on three layers:

## 1. Pillar Pages
High-authority cornerstone content targeting broad commercial or informational intent.

Examples:
- Ecommerce SEO Strategy
- Shopify SEO Guide
- Business Credit Pillar

These pages accumulate:
- backlinks
- topical relevance
- engagement signals
- semantic trust

---

## 2. Cluster Pages
Supporting content targeting narrower long-tail queries.

Examples:
- collection page SEO
- product schema
- AI SEO tools
- Core Web Vitals

These pages reinforce the pillar while capturing additional search intent.

---

## 3. Revenue Pages
Pages directly tied to monetization:
- products
- services
- calculators
- lead funnels

Authority is intentionally pushed toward these pages through structured internal linking.

---

# Core Principle

Random internal linking creates crawl chaos.

Intentional internal linking creates authority concentration.

The Authority Flow Model™ focuses on:
- semantic relationships
- crawl efficiency
- keyword ownership
- topical reinforcement
- authority consolidation
- conversion pathways

The goal is not simply traffic.

The goal is controlled authority movement toward revenue-generating assets.
