## Editorial Use

Semrush should support strategy claims, not replace firsthand experience.

Best use:
“Semrush’s SEO research and keyword workflow resources support the importance of matching content to search intent and auditing competitive SERPs before building content.”

Avoid:
fake statistics, unsupported percentage claims, or outdated numbers without checking the live source.
