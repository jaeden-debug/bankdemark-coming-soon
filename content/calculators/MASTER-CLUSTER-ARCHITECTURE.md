## GLOBAL INTERNAL LINK RULES

### Every Article Must Link To:
1. Homepage (/ ) — anchor: "BankDeMark"
2. /financial-calculators — anchor: "financial calculators"
3. Its own cluster pillar — anchor: primary keyword of that pillar
4. /command — anchor: "Command by BankDeMark" or "financial dashboard"
5. Minimum 2 other calculators by contextual anchor text
6. Minimum 1 supporting article within the same cluster

### Pillar → Supporting (Mandatory)
Every pillar links down to ALL supporting articles in its cluster.

### Supporting → Pillar (Mandatory)
Every supporting article links upward to its cluster pillar (minimum 3 times).

### Cross-Cluster Links (High Priority)
- Compound Interest ↔ Investment Calculator (compounding is how investments grow)
- Retirement ↔ FIRE (retirement is a prerequisite concept for FIRE)
- Net Worth ↔ Debt Payoff (net worth increases as debt decreases)
- Budget Calculator ↔ Debt Payoff (cash flow funds debt repayment)
- FIRE ↔ Retirement (FIRE is accelerated retirement)
- Compound Interest ↔ RRSP/TFSA (compounding inside registered accounts)
