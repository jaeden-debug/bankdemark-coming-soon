# Blackwater Aquatics Live Scuds Ranking Case Study

## Starting Point

The live scuds product was the highest-value SEO target for Blackwater Aquatics.

The product had commercial intent, low Canadian competition, and strong customer fit.

Main target searches included:

- live scuds Canada
- scuds for sale
- buy live scuds Canada
- freshwater scuds
- scuds for aquarium
- scuds for betta fish

## Problem

The page needed more than a product listing.

Google needed supporting proof that Blackwater Aquatics understood:

- what scuds are
- how scuds are used
- why fish eat scuds
- how to culture scuds
- whether scuds are safe
- how scuds compare to frozen food and daphnia

Without that support, the product page was isolated.

## Strategy

The scud strategy used the Compound SEO Engine™:

- product page optimization
- “where to buy live scuds in Canada” guide
- “how to culture live scuds” guide
- “are scuds good for fish” support article
- “scuds vs frozen food” comparison article
- “what do scuds eat” guide
- “scuds for pea puffers” article
- “self-sustaining aquarium scuds” article
- internal links back to the scud product page

## Results

The scud page became a core SEO asset for the site.

Observed outcomes:

- scud-related queries appeared in Google Search Console
- the scud product page became one of the top organic pages
- long-tail query impressions increased
- informational articles began supporting product intent
- product visibility improved without relying heavily on paid ads

## Operator Observation

This is where niche SEO gets powerful.

A large pet store can sell fish food.

But a focused niche brand can own the specific search intent around live scuds, freshwater amphipods, fry nutrition, and natural feeding behavior.
