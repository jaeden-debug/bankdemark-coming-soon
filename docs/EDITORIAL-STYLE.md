# BankDeMark Editorial Style

## Goal

Content should feel:
- authoritative
- operator-level
- strategic
- experience-based
- human
- opinionated when appropriate

NOT:
- robotic
- over-polished
- corporate
- generic AI SEO content

---

# Writing Rules

## 1. Use Rhythm Variation

Mix:
- short paragraphs
- medium paragraphs
- occasional longer sections

Avoid perfectly balanced structure.

---

## 2. Inject Human Friction

Not every sentence should transition smoothly.

Humans interrupt rhythm naturally.

Example:
“Most stores completely ignore this.

That becomes a serious problem later.”

---

## 3. Use Operator Observations

Include:
- real-world patterns
- observations
- frustrations
- lessons learned
- strategic insights

Example:
“Google can detect topical chaos surprisingly quickly once a site scales.”

---

## 4. Add Contrarian Insights

Avoid generic consensus writing.

Example:
“Publishing more articles is not automatically an SEO strategy.”

---

## 5. Use Stronger Opinions Carefully

Examples:
- “Most ecommerce SEO audits are surface-level.”
- “Thin collection pages quietly kill rankings.”
- “Traffic without conversion intent is mostly vanity.”

---

## 6. Avoid AI Fingerprints

Avoid:
- repetitive cadence
- perfect transitions
- excessive symmetry
- robotic consistency
- repetitive sentence structures

---

## 7. Prioritize Demonstrated Experience

Whenever possible:
- use real examples
- use real observations
- use screenshots
- use metrics
- use case studies

Google increasingly rewards demonstrated experience.

---

## 8. Build Semantic Consistency

Recurring branded systems:
- Compound SEO Engine™
- Revenue-Weighted SEO™
- Authority Flow Model™
- Search Intent Ladder™
- Organic Asset Framework™

These concepts should recur naturally across content.
